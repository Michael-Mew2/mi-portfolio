Some Places I used to Work

UEFA EURO 2024

Summer of 2024

Volunteer

This summer, I had the opportunity to volunteer at the UEFA EURO 2024 in Hamburg, where I supported visitors with their questions and needs, making sure everyone had a positive experience. I also helped lead workshops for kids, creating a fun and engaging environment to introduce them to the sport. It was a fantastic experience to be part of such an international event, connecting with fans and fostering enthusiasm for football!

Bremerhavener Formularlotsen

Still Working

Volunteer

As a volunteer with Bremerhaven's 'Formularlotsen,' I get to support people who need assistance with applications for things like child benefits, citizen’s income, and housing aid. Beyond that, I’m focused on giving our organization a fresh, more visible identity to help us reach even more people. I’m working on a new website and updating our look across platforms, including WhatsApp, so the Formularlotsen are easier to find and recognize in the community.

S&S Transporte

Used to Work

Minijob

From April 2020 to November 2024, I worked as a Junior Marketing Manager (part-time) at S&S Transporte. I designed, built, and managed the company’s website, transforming it from a simple digital business card into a customer-friendly platform for direct communication. I also redesigned the corporate identity (CI) for stronger brand recognition. In addition, I optimized the website’s SEO, managed the company’s social media channels, and created engaging social media content and visuals to boost online presence and customer engagement. This role provided me with valuable experience in web development, digital marketing, and content creation.

Deutsches Auswanderer-
haus

Used to Work

Internship

During my internship at the "Deutsches Auswandererhaus", I had the opportunity to be involved in organizing a variety of exciting events, including the "Long Night of Culture" and the opening of a new special exhibition. I was also responsible for daily marketing tasks, such as creating press kits, writing promotional content, and developing concepts for implementing future technologies like AR in the museum. This involved exploring the potential impact of such technologies on finances, visitor numbers, and overall guest experience. It was an incredible experience to blend creativity with strategy, helping to shape both the museum's present and future.
